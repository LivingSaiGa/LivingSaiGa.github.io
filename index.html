Project Description

We are activists addressing society on taking accountability for our environment. Our role is a communal obligation, an action to maintain and support unique diversities that our planet has to offer.

Through peaceful demonstration, we aim to liberate our society in rethinking behaviour. To consider the repercussions of our actions and to grow as proactive, conscientious members of our communities.

Using companion planting methods, we occupy public spaces with mobile vegetable gardens. Species of disappearing vegetables are purposefully planted to reinforce the urgency of change that our environment, of which we cannot live in a world without, so rightfully demands.
